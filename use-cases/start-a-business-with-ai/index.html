Workflow: From Idea to Launch

1

Validate Your Idea

Don't build yet. Ask ChatGPT or Claude to critique your business model. "Act as a VC. Roast my idea for [X]. What are the risks? Who are the competitors?"

Goal: Find the flaws before they cost you money.

2

Generate Brand Assets

Use Looka or Midjourney for logos. Use ChatGPT to generate names, slogans, and brand voice guidelines.

Time saved: professional branding in 1 hour vs 2 weeks.

3

Build the Website / Landing Page

Use AI warsite builders like Framer AI or Durable to generate a full site structure, copy, and images in seconds. Refine manually.

Alternative: Use Relume to generate a wireframe and export to Figma/Webflow.

4

Build the Product (MVP)

Non-technical? Use Bolt.new or Lovable to generate full web apps from text prompts. "Build a dashboard that tracks X."

See our Build SaaS Guide for details.

5

Create Marketing Content

Generate a month of social posts, blogs, and ad copy using Jasper or Copy.ai. Use Sora/Runway for video ads.

6

Automate Operations

Set up Zapier or Make to handle leads. "When new form submission → add to CRM → send welcome email."

Mistakes Founders Make with AI

1. Trusting AI Market Research Blindly

The Fix: AI hallucinates numbers. Use it for logic and strategy, but verify market size and competitor data manually.

2. Generating Generic Branding

The Fix: Don't use the first logo option. iterate. Refine. "Make it more modern, less generic." Push the tools.

3. Building Without Talking to Users

The Fix: AI can build the product, but it can't be the customer. You still need to talk to real humans to validate fit.

4. Over-Automating Support Early

The Fix: Don't put an AI chatbot in front of your first 100 customers. Talk to them yourself to learn what's broken.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI really write code for me?

Yes. Tools like Bolt and Cursor are incredibly capable. However, you will still need to act as a "Product Manager" to direct them clearly.

Is the content copyright free?

Generally yes, but laws are evolving. US Copyright Office says AI-generated work cannot be copyrighted. Use AI to assist, but add your huge human touch.

Which AI is best for business plans?

Claude 3.5 Sonnet is currently widely regarded as the best model for reasoning, strategy, and long-form writing.
