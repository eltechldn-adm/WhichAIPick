FAQ

Do you test every tool?

No. We index tools and provide basic information to help you discover options. We don't claim to have personally tested everything.

How do you make money?

Some links are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if you click through and make a purchase. This doesn't affect your price. See our disclosure page.

How does the quiz work?

The AI Tool Quiz asks about your needs and budget to suggest relevant tools. It's a simple filter, not a definitive recommendation.

How often is this updated?

We add and update tools regularly. Category assignments and tool details are ongoing work.

Can I submit a tool?

Not yet, but we're considering it. For now, use the contact link in our About page if you have suggestions.
