Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how WhichAIPick uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

How We Use Cookies

WhichAIPick may use cookies for:

  • Essential Functionality: To remember your preferences and maintain basic site functionality
  • Analytics: To understand how visitors use our website and improve our content
  • Performance: To monitor site performance and technical issues

We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking across other websites.

Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies

Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used for basic site navigation.

Persistent Cookies

Cookies that remain on your device for a set period. Used to remember your preferences across visits.

Third-Party Cookies

If we use analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics), these services may set their own cookies. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

Managing Cookies

You have control over cookies through your browser settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Delete cookies after each session
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Please note that blocking cookies may affect your experience on our website.

Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies, consult your browser's help section:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices evolve. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
