Report Misuse

Help us maintain a quality directory by reporting inaccurate, misleading, or inappropriate tool listings.

What to Report

Please report tool listings that contain:

  • Inaccurate Information: Wrong tool name, description, or category
  • Broken Links: Tool website URLs that don't work
  • Misleading Content: Descriptions that misrepresent what the tool does
  • Spam or Malicious Content: Listings that link to harmful websites
  • Duplicate Entries: The same tool listed multiple times
  • Inappropriate Content: Listings that violate our content standards

How to Report

To report a problematic listing, please email us at:

Email: hello@whichaipick.com

Information to Include

Please provide the following in your report:

  • Tool name and URL from our directory
  • Specific issue you're reporting
  • Description of the problem
  • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, links, etc.)

What Happens Next

When you submit a report:

  1. We'll review your submission within 3-5 business days
  2. We'll investigate the listed tool and verify the issue
  3. If the issue is confirmed, we'll update or remove the listing
  4. We may contact you if we need clarification

Our Standards

WhichAIPick maintains quality standards for all tool listings. We expect that:

  • Tool information is accurate and up-to-date
  • Descriptions honestly represent tool capabilities
  • Links lead to legitimate, functional websites
  • Tools are categorized appropriately

False Reports

Please only submit legitimate concerns. Repeated false or malicious reports may result in your reports being ignored.

Other Concerns

For issues unrelated to tool listings, please visit our Contact page.

Thank You

We appreciate your help in maintaining the quality of WhichAIPick. Your reports help us provide accurate, reliable information to all users.
